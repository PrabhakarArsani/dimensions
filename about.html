Our Story

Dimensions Doors and Windows Systems is a leading manufacturer of premium uPVC and Aluminium profile systems. Dimensions Doors and Windows Systems are proud to be authorized partners with Sudhakar uPVC, one of India's most trusted names in the door and window industry. With years of experience in the industry, Dimensions Doors and Windows Systems have established ourselves as a trusted name in providing high-quality door and window solutions for both residential and commercial projects.
